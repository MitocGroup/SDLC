Stela Lupusor

Stela Lupushor is a renowned change agent with the mission to humanize the workplace and who has been working with leading organizations to redefine their work environment for over 20 years.

Stela is the founder of Reframe.Work Inc., a consulting firm advising organizations on how to future-proof their work environment in light of demographic shifts, disruptive technology, value chain disintermediation and human behavior evolution. She also serves as the Program Director, Strategic Workforce Planning Council at The Conference Board, facilitating conversations with the leaders of workforce planning at Fortune 500 corporations on building global workforce strategies to deliver business performance.

She is the co-founder of the Strategic HR Analytics Meet-up group bringing together over 900 members in the NYC area to evolve the people analytics field. She is also the Founder, Chairperson of the Board of Directors of Amazing.Community - a non-profit organization with the goal to equip aging women with skills and support so they can stay in the workforce.

Previously Stela led the People Analytics functions at Fidelity Investments and TIAA, led the HR Strategy and Social Analytics function at IBM where she built their "future of work" strategy, and also held various management consulting roles advising the clients of Price Waterhouse, PricewaterhouseCoopers and IBM with human capital and digital transformations.

Stela holds a degree in Mathematics and Computer Science, speaks three languages, has a patent pending for a social sentiment analysis tool, has published articles and papers, and is a sought-after speaker on the topics of People Analytics and the Future of Work.
