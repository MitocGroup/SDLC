404 Moldova is a coworking space for IT businesses and IT people from Moldova. We want to empower local IT community of small companies and skilled individuals to be competitive globally. Members of our community are proud to be working with Fortune 500 customers from Europe and USA.

This coworking space is capable to host at least 50 individuals and is an important step towards the development of IT industry in Moldova. Entrepreneurs and freelancers will find here modern offices, fully loaded kitchen and several conference rooms.

According to Managing Director of "404 Moldova", Vladimir Ursu, this center was created to help developers and businesses come together into an amazing IT community. As part of this platform, members of this community will develop their skills and gather experience to become more competitive locally and globally.

"We are planning to organize different events, training sessions and team buildings to grow the skills and experience of our members in new innovative technologies like Cloud Computing and Serverless Architectures", said Vladimir Ursu.

Head Economic Development at USAid Moldova, Daniel Thomson, mentioned that his agency is actively supporting Moldova's IT industry for many years. Constant growth and development of IT sector is the main success factor for country's economy and sustainable transformation through technology adoption and innovation.

"This new coworking space for IT professionals “404 Moldova” is aligning Moldova with international trends in IT industry and is placing this country on international map of most attractive destinations for IT companies. Young professionals and startups around the world will discover new opportunities to develop their business here, while local IT workforce will be able to work on most competitive and important global markets without leaving their home country", said Daniel Thomson.
